Lab Tasks

  • 1 Move the compass around the magnet to observe field direction.
  • 2 Observe the magnetic field lines inside the bar magnet.
  • 3 Switch to "Planet Earth" mode to see how a compass helps in navigation.

Pro Tip

The compass needle's red end always points towards the Magnetic North. Try changing the magnet strength and see how the field lines respond!
